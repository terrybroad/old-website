Slitscanning Experiments

Experiments with different geometric variations on the tried and tested technique of slit-scanning from a first person perspective. All the videos were made using custom C++ software and led to my work on a commision for Converse.

Video footage was from a collaborative project that was directed by Yoni Lappin, shot by Adam Scarth and supported by Agile films. Unfortunately the original project never made the light of day, but the footage got put to good use in these experiments.

This project was featured on Prosthetic Knowledge.
