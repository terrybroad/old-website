About Me

I am an artist and research student currently studying in the Department of Computing at Goldsmiths, University of London. My current research is exploring the creative applications of deep learning technologies, and investigating their generative capabilities. Previously, I have researched methods for editing light field photographs and exploring alternative sensory experiences with augmented reality.

I worked for online open hardware platform Wevolver, writing instruction manuals for robots and other cool projects, during my time there I also contributed to their philanthropic side-project Robots for Good. I also spent a summer interning at digital design studio FutureDeluxe as a creative technologist.

I will be graduating this summer (2016) so will looking for full time work or internship placements in the near future. I also work as a freelance programmer and creative technologist. I am very keen to collaborate on cool projects so feel free to contact me if you have an exciting idea or need my services.

You can find me on github, twitter, linkedIn and medium.
